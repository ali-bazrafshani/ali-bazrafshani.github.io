Homepage

I am a researcher in robotics with a strong background in mechanical design, soft actuation, and aerial platforms. My current work focuses on combining nature-inspired design and learning-based control methods.

I completed my Master's in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Tehran and have contributed to several UAV and SAM system development projects. My goal is to build the next generation of adaptive, soft, and intelligent robots.

My research interests include robotic manipulation, soft robotics, and bio-inspired UAVs. Outside the lab, I enjoy hiking, photography, and playing Othello.

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in my work (or in my ice cream 🍨).
